# Introduction: Your Digital Memory Brain

Welcome to the **Neural Image Memory System** (NIMS). Think of this not as a "photo gallery," but as a **digital brain** that remembers your life as you lived it.

This documentation is designed to be understood at three levels:
1.  **The Essence**: Simple analogies for everyone.
2.  **The Logic**: How the system "thinks."
3.  **The Engineering**: High-level technical details for developers.

---

## 1. What is NIMS? (The Essence)

Imagine if your photo collection could talk back to you. 

- **Traditional Gallery**: It's a box of photos. You search for "beach," and it shows you sand.
- **NIMS**: It's a living journal. You ask, "Who was I with when I felt most relaxed last summer?" and it remembers that you were with your sister at the coast because it understands **who** people are and **why** they matter to you.

---

## 2. How it Works (The Logic)

NIMS follows a simple loop: **Watch → Remember → Connect → Recall**.

1.  **Watch (Analysis)**: The system "looks" at your photos using artificial eyes. It sees people, objects, and text.
2.  **Remember (Vaulting)**: It stores these details in a secure vault. It doesn't just store pixels; it stores the *meaning* of the moment.
3.  **Connect (Relationship Building)**: Over time, the system realizes that the "unknown man" in photo A is the same "father" in photo B. It builds a social map of your life automatically.
4.  **Recall (The Assistant)**: You talk to your memories using our AI Chat. It uses everything it has learned to answer your questions perfectly.

---

## 3. The Power Under the Hood (The Engineering)

For those building with us, NIMS is a complex orchestration of several modern technologies:

- **AI Eyes (VLM)**: Uses Google Gemini to "understand" images as deeply as a human would.
- **Micro-Details (Multi-Pass)**: It zooms in on faces and clothes to make sure it recognizes your friends every time.
- **Memory Context (RAG)**: When you chat, the system builds a text-based "briefing" of your entire life's memories so the AI can answer accurately.
- **Atomic Safety**: It saves your data in a way that it can never be "half-saved" or corrupted, like an digital indestructible safe.
